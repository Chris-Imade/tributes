Announcement of Transition


It is with deep sorrow but gratitude to God for a life well spent that we announce the passing on of our beloved father, Bishop Dr. Felix Onomeregbor, who transitioned to glory on Saturday, the 21st of December 2024.


He was a Cherished father, seasoned Prophet, and friend, fondly called SHAMMAH and the Omraho 1 of Olomu Kingdom, whose legacy of love, faith, and dedication continues to inspire us.


We ask for your prayers and support during this time as we honor his life and cherish the beautiful memories he leaves behind. Further details regarding his funeral arrangements will be communicated in due course.


Thank you for standing with us during this moment.


Yours Sincerely,
His Children

His Life & Legacy

Bishop Dr. Felix was a beacon of light, touching countless lives with his wisdom, compassion, and unwavering faith. As SHAMMAH and Omraho 1 of Olomu Kingdom, his impact extended far beyond the pulpit, creating a legacy of love, service, and spiritual guidance that continues to inspire all who knew him.
